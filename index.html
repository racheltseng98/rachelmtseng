Community Involvement

Over Winter Break 2019, a group of 10 from Sheil pioneered a service trip to the Mexican border in Arizona. We listened, prayed, and helped organizations that were dedicated to helping asylum seekers in whatever way we could. We also had the opportunity to witness a naturalization ceremony. One of the biggest takeaways that I hold dear to my heart is: 'Being Present Is The Most Powerful Expression Of Love.'
